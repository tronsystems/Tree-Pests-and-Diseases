Tree Disease and Pest Reporter

Chalara dieback of ash is a serious disease of ash trees. It causes leaf loss, especially in the crown of the tree, and will usually be fatal. Tracking diseased trees will improve our understanding of Chalara and help identify resistant strains. You can help by reading the advice here and reporting any sightings.
