Chalara

Chalara fraxinea
(Ash Dieback)

Chalara dieback of ash is a serious disease of ash trees caused by a fungus called Chalara fraxinea. The disease causes leaf loss and crown dieback in affected trees, and it can kill trees.

You'll find information below on how to prevent spreading the disease, identifying ash trees, spotting signs of the disease, details on how to report any sightings.
