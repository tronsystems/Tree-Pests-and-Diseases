Tree Disease and Pest Reporter

Please report sightings of tree diseases and pests to help contain their spread.

The Forestry Commission is the government department responsible for the protection and expansion of Britain's forests and woodlands.

Our main focus at present is on 'Ash Dieback' (Chalara fraxinea) but you may also report sightings of other diseases and pests such as 'Sudden Oak Death' or the 'Oak Processionary Moth'. Report a Sighting
